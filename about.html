About Us

At Thursday Cafe, we believe in creating unique and memorable experiences for our customers. Our story began in the heart of Antipolo City, where we decided to bring our passion for baking and love for beverages to the community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, customizable cakes and a wide variety of beverages to enhance every celebration. We are dedicated to using only the finest ingredients and ensuring each product is made with love and care.

Our Vision

We envision Thursday Cafe as a place where every customer feels valued and delighted with their experience. We aim to be a part of your special moments, making them even more memorable with our unique offerings.

Our Values

  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our ingredients.
  • Customer Satisfaction: We strive to exceed our customers' expectations.
  • Creativity: We believe in the power of creativity to make every cake special.
  • Community: We are committed to being a positive part of our community.
